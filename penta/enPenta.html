PENTA Apartments offers accommodation in 5 spacious apartments equipped with premium hotel standards.

Architecture

PENTA Apartments surprises even the most exigent tourists through the perfect detailing and the elegance of the traditional mountain architecture.

Ideal position

Small luxury location, placed in the forest nearby, close to the main tourist attractions , and close to the city center of Sinaia.

Mountain view

All villa's apartments are offering a great mountain view, to the forest and also to the historical centre of Sinaia.

Accomodation

Accomodation services in 5 different spacious appartments with premium hotel standards, each of them having an unique and modern design.

Self-service

Check-in/check-out self-services, access codes in all spaces, adjustable temperatures in each room, electronics of the latest generation.

A luxury mall-like location where every detail is carefully thought out, the exceptional architecture of the villa and each apartment, the enchanting atmosphere – all are available to provide you with a memorable experience and a well-deserved break. Whether you want to relax in a classic, romantic, or intimate atmosphere, or if you prefer to enjoy mountain activities or explore local attractions, Penta Apartments is the perfect place.
